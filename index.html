1. Overview

Dolphin Link is a mobile application designed to help users scan URLs and QR codes, detect phishing attempts, and ensure link safety. We are committed to protecting your privacy and do not collect any personally identifiable information without your knowledge.

2. Information We Collect

  • Scanned URLs or QR Code Data: When you scan a QR code or enter a URL, the data may be sent to a secure remote server for real-time phishing analysis. This data is not stored or shared.
  • Camera Access: We request camera access strictly to enable QR code scanning. No photos or videos are recorded or stored.
  • Internet Access: Required to fetch results from external threat analysis services and show link safety reports.

3. AdMob and Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display ads within the app. AdMob may collect certain anonymous data to serve relevant ads, including:

  • Device information (such as OS version, language, and screen size)
  • General location data (non-precise)
  • Advertising ID

To learn more, please visit the Google Privacy Policy.

4. How We Use Information

The limited information collected is used to:

  • Analyze links and detect phishing threats
  • Display scan results and advertisements

We do not collect or store personal information such as names, emails, or contact lists.

5. Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party APIs to analyze URLs (e.g., phishing databases or malware scanners). These services process URLs temporarily and do not retain user data.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data, but no internet-based service is 100% secure.

7. Children’s Privacy

Dolphin Link is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. All updates will be posted here with a revised "Last Updated" date.
